How to Get There

Directions

Address:
Hidden Oakes, Stoneygate Lane, Ribchester, PR3 3YN

  • By Car: Easily reached from the M6. Follow signs to Ribchester, then take Stoneygate Lane. Look for the Oak Bank Farm sign and black gates. Free parking on site.
  • By Train: The nearest stations are Whalley or Clitheroe, a short taxi ride from the church.

  • By Bus: Local buses run from Clitheroe to Hurst Green, a short taxi ride from the village.
